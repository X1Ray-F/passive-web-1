Passive Web curates concise, useful guides and templates that help freelancers, makers, and travelers make decisions faster. Every page is designed to be skimmable, actionable, and monetized transparently through PayPal for simple, low-friction purchases.

## Mission
- Ship lightweight digital products that save people time.
- Keep articles short, structured, and updated for 2026 realities.
- Monetize ethically with clear calls to action and no gated basics.

## What you can expect
- Pilot content across three niches: freelance templates, 3D printing troubleshooting, and eSIM travel tips.
- Downloadable resources and spreadsheets sized for solo operators.
- Clear interlinking so you can move from problem to solution quickly.
